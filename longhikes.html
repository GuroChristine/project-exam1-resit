Long hikes

These are the most extreme of all our extreme hikes, and are usually two
weeks or longer. That include trekking, local culture and food, as well as
nature exclusions. These are our physically demanding hikes, so if you
are planning to do one of these you should be in reasonably good physical shape.

Larapinta Trail, Australia

14 650 kr

A 12-day trak on the Larapinta trail starting from and concluding at
Alice Springs.

Patagonia, Argentina/Chile

36 500 kr

A two-week intense tour, including Refugio Frey, Cerro Castillo, and
explorades glacier. This is a demanding tour, but well worth the effort!

Jammu and Kashmir, India

45 000 kr

Some of the most breathtaking views of northern India are included on
this three-week tour, with an increasingly demanding hiking itinerary.
