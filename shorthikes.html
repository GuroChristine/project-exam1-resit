Short hikes

Our short hikes are perfect for weekend getaway or team building trips.
Normally these are day trips from a local base, and do not require
camping equipment.

Weekend in the Pyrénées

9500kr

Wildlife park parc naturel Régional des Pyrénées Catalanes. The trip
includes a visit to a Cathar castle as well as an exhibition of local birds of
pray.

Seven mountains, Bergen

4500kr

The “seven mountains” are the peaks surrounding the Norwegian city of
Bergen hiking club offers an annual tour og these. Be sure to bring rain
gear.

Monte Capanne, Tuscany

11 000 kr

Located on the island of Elba, Monte Capanne is one of the highest peaks
in Tuscany. Visit the Hermitage of San Cerbone, as well as the fort near
the medieval village, which is over 800 years old. Spectacular views of
other islands of the archipelago, including Corsica, from the summit.
